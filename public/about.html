The ChenOne Journey

Founded in 2010 by Chen Wei, a passionate designer and stationery enthusiast, ChenOne began as a small boutique shop in downtown Singapore. Chen's vision was simple yet profound: to create a place where quality meets creativity.

Starting with just 50 carefully selected products, we've grown into a trusted brand serving thousands of customers worldwide. Our commitment to excellence has never wavered, and our passion for beautiful, functional stationery continues to drive us forward.

Today, ChenOne partners with the world's finest manufacturers to bring you an exclusive collection of pens, notebooks, art supplies, and desk accessories that inspire creativity and enhance productivity.
